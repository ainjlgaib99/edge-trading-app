TRADE MANAGEMENT
What you do after you're in — three rules only
01
Breakeven Rule
Once price moves 1R in your direction — move stop to entry. You cannot lose this trade. Lock it in.
02
Partial Exit
At first target (1R or POC), take 50% off the position. Let the rest run to full target. Secure profit, ride the rest.
03
Chop Exit
If price stalls mid-range and starts chopping sideways, close it. A small win beats a winner turned loser.
❌ The One Rule You Never Break
Never move your stop further away because "it might come back." Your original stop was your maximum risk. Moving it away is how small losses become account-killing losses. The market does not care about your feelings.
Once you're in a trade, there are only three things you can do: hold, take partial, or close. That's it. There is no "let me see what happens" — that's how you lose. If you don't have a plan before you enter, you don't enter.
