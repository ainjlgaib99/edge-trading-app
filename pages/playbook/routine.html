MORNING ROUTINE
What you do before 6:30 AM — every single day
⏰ Start This 45 Minutes Before 6:30 AM
No social media. No financial news. No distractions. Your mind needs to be clear before you look at price.
1
Open TradingView — 30 min chart on NQ or ES. Look at the overnight session. Where did price go? Did it make new highs or new lows? Where did it settle? The overnight range shows you where institutions were positioning.
2
Pull your Fixed Range Volume Profile (FRVP) from yesterday's RTH session — 9:30 AM to 4:00 PM close. Mark three horizontal lines: VAH (top of value), VAL (bottom of value), POC (fattest node = most volume). These are your levels for the day.
3
Check the economic calendar for news events. If CPI, FOMC, NFP, or any major release is scheduled at or before 8 AM — you close the platform and come back tomorrow. News destroys your levels.
4
Mark yesterday's high and yesterday's low as additional reference lines. Price gravitates to these too.
5
At 6:30 AM — watch only. Do not touch anything for the first 15 minutes minimum. Let the market show you where it wants to go before you commit capital.
