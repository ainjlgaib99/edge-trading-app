WHEN TO STAY OUT
Sitting on your hands is a trade
The best traders sit on their hands 80% of the time. Staying out when conditions are bad is how you protect your account. Every single item below means zero trades today.
✕
Major news event within 30 minutes of your session — CPI, FOMC, NFP, jobless claims. News makes your levels meaningless.
✕
Price is in the middle of the value area — no edge here, pure chop. You only trade at levels, not away from them.
✕
You already took 2 trades today — doesn't matter if they won or lost. 2 trades is your daily limit. Close the platform.
✕
You already lost twice today — hard stop, no exceptions. You are done. Walk away. No revenge trading.
✕
You are emotional — angry from a loss, overconfident from a win, distracted, tired, or in a bad headspace. Emotions cost money.
✕
Overnight range is extremely wide (100+ points on NQ) — the market is already volatile and your levels are less reliable.
✕
You don't have a clear level to trade from — if you are guessing where to enter, you are out. No guessing.
The Right Mindset
You do not need to trade every day to make money. A week with 4 good trades and 0 bad ones beats a week with 15 trades and blown risk. Patience is literally the edge.
