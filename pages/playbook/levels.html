PICKING LEVELS
How to build your map the night before
After 4 PM when RTH closes, open TradingView. Your job takes 10 minutes. Do this every trading night — it becomes automatic.
1
Set your Fixed Range Volume Profile from 9:30 AM open to 4:00 PM close (today's RTH session). You'll see the full volume distribution.
2
Identify and write down three numbers:
VAH — top of the value area (where 70% of volume was traded)
VAL — bottom of the value area
POC — the single highest-volume price level (fattest bar)
3
Mark today's high and today's low as horizontal lines. These are tomorrow's reference points too.
4
On Mondays and Fridays — also pull the weekly FRVP. Weekly VAH and VAL are even stronger magnetic levels than daily ones.
The Core Principle
Price does not move randomly. It auctions up to find sellers and auctions down to find buyers. VAH is where sellers were active. VAL is where buyers were active. POC is where the most agreement happened. Tomorrow price will return to test these levels again. That's your edge.
The rule: You only take trades when price is at or very near one of your marked levels. Away from levels = no trade. If price is in the middle of the value area, you are watching, not trading.
