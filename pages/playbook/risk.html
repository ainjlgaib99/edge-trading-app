RISK RULES
The math that keeps you in the game
These are not suggestions. Breaking risk rules is the single fastest way to blow an account or fail an eval.
Standard Risk Per Trade
$200–250. On a $50K Topstep eval the max drawdown is ~$2,500. Lose 2 trades at $250 each = $500 down. Still well within limits. Still calm. Still able to recover without emotion.
Max Risk — High Conviction Only
$500 maximum. Only on the clearest, cleanest setups — maybe one per week qualifies. Not every trade is $500. Most are $200–250.
How to calculate your stop in ticks:
MNQ — $2 per tick
$200 risk ÷ $2/tick = 100 ticks stop distance
$250 risk ÷ $2/tick = 125 ticks stop distance
$500 risk ÷ $2/tick = 250 ticks stop distance
MES — $5 per tick (0.25 points)
$200 risk ÷ $5/tick = 40 ticks stop distance
$250 risk ÷ $5/tick = 50 ticks stop distance
$500 risk ÷ $5/tick = 100 ticks stop distance
Hard Rule
If your stop requires more ticks than your risk allows — the setup is not valid. Skip it. Do not widen your risk to fit the trade. Let the trade pass.
